Romeo & Juliet


Production Company: WildRice Theatre Company

Information

WildRice, known for their fresh and dynamic interpretations of classic works, has brought a modern flair to Romeo and Juliet while honoring Shakespeare’s original language and themes. This production promises breathtaking visuals, striking costumes, and a set design that transports audiences straight to the heart of Verona. The artistic direction combines contemporary and classic elements, making this a must-see for both longtime fans of the play and newcomers alike.

While Cast Eyes Theatre Company isn’t directly producing this show, we’re thrilled to provide our venue for this incredible reimagining of Romeo and Juliet. Our mission is to support a wide range of theatrical productions, welcoming external companies like WildRice to bring diverse stories to life on our stage.
