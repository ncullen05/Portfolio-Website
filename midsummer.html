A Midsummer Night's Dream


Production Company: Cast Eyes Theatre Company

Information

Step into an enchanting world of mischief, magic, and romance with Cast Eyes Theatre Company’s vibrant production of A Midsummer Night’s Dream. Set in a whimsical reimagining of Athens and its mystical woods, our interpretation of Shakespeare’s timeless comedy combines stunning visuals, dynamic performances, and an original score to transport you to a realm where reality blurs with fantasy.

Follow the intertwining tales of star-crossed lovers, feuding fairies, and hapless actors as their fates collide under the glow of a midsummer moon. Directed by Andrew Nielsen, this production celebrates the playful spirit of Shakespeare’s masterpiece while exploring themes of love, transformation, and the power of dreams.
