Othello


Production Company: Cast Eyes Theatre Company

Information

Experience Shakespeare’s timeless tragedy like never before in Cast Eyes Theatre Company’s bold new production of Othello. Directed by the visionary Flavio Boccelli, this reimagining brings passion, betrayal, and heartbreak to the forefront with a modern flair while remaining true to the play's classic roots.

Starring the renowned comedian and actor Jack Whitehall as the tormented and manipulative Iago, and beloved broadcaster Richard Hammond making his theatrical debut as the valiant yet vulnerable Othello, this production promises a captivating blend of humor, intensity, and raw emotion.

Set against a backdrop of political intrigue and personal betrayal, Othello explores themes of love, jealousy, and the devastating consequences of unchecked ambition. With Flavio Boccelli’s innovative direction, expect a visually stunning and emotionally charged performance that breathes fresh life into one of Shakespeare's most powerful works.

Join us as we delve into the complexities of loyalty and deceit in a production that showcases Cast Eyes Theatre Company's commitment to pushing the boundaries of contemporary theatre. Don’t miss this extraordinary event!
