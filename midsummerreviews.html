Reviews

Ann Garfield (The Irish Times, 2023): “Pat Shortt’s mischievously brilliant Puck steals the show in Cast Eyes Theatre Company’s A Midsummer Night’s Dream, a production bursting with whimsy under Andrew Nielsen’s inspired direction. Cillian Murphy’s brooding Lysander offers a compelling counterpoint to the comedy, while Meryl Streep’s regal and witty Philostrate elevates every scene she graces. With dazzling visuals and a dreamlike atmosphere, this production is pure magic from start to finish."

Patricia Hayes (The Sunday Independent, 2023): "Cast Eyes Theatre Company’s A Midsummer Night’s Dream, helmed by Andrew Nielsen, delivers an enchanting spectacle, though it occasionally sacrifices subtlety for grandeur. Pat Shortt’s energetic Puck is hilariously magnetic, and Cillian Murphy’s intense Lysander brings unexpected depth, though his seriousness contrasts with the play’s lighter elements. Meryl Streep’s Philostrate is a masterclass in making every moment count, lending gravitas to a smaller role in a visually ambitious production."

Tim Murray (The Sun, 2022): “Andrew Nielsen’s direction brings Shakespeare’s forest of mischief to vivid life in Cast Eyes Theatre Company’s delightful A Midsummer Night’s Dream. Pat Shortt’s riotous Puck kept the audience in stitches, while Cillian Murphy’s Lysander and Meryl Streep’s Philostrate delivered performances of surprising emotional range and commanding presence. The enchanting design and flawless execution ensure this is a dream that audiences won’t soon forget.”
